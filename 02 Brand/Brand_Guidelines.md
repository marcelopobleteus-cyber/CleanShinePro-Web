# CleanShine Pro - Brand Guidelines

> "Professional Cleaning. Corporate Standards."

## 1. Brand Identity
**CleanShine Pro** is a premium commercial and residential cleaning company focused on standardized quality, reliability, and compliance.
- **Promise**: Limpieza profesional con estándares corporativos.
- **Tagline**: Commercial & Residential Facility Services.

## 2. Logo System
### Primary Logo
The Shield + Sparkle design represents protection, hygiene, and brilliance.
- **Files**: `CleanShinePro_Logo_Primary.svg`
- **Usage**: Website headers, proposals, truck decals.

### Variations
- **Vertical**: For uniforms and social media profiles.
- **Icon Only**: Favicons, app icons, stickers.
- **Monochrome Black**: Invoices, stamps, embroidery.
- **Monochrome White**: On dark backgrounds (Navy/Charcoal), vehicle windows.

### Usage Rules
- **Clear Space**: Leave a margin equal to the height of the "C" in CleanShine around the logo.
- **Minimum Size**: 
    - Web: 160px width.
    - Print: 35mm width.
- **Don'ts**:
    - Do not stretch or distort.
    - Do not change colors.
    - Do not add heavy dropshadows.

## 3. Color Palette
### Primary Colors
- **Corporate Navy** (`#0A2E5C`): Trust, authority, depth. Used for headers, backgrounds.
- **Hygiene Emerald** (`#00A878`): Freshness, action, "Go". Used for buttons, checkmarks.
- **White** (`#FFFFFF`): Cleanliness base.

### Neutrals
- **Charcoal** (`#2C2C2C`): Text.
- **Cool Gray** (`#6B7280`): Subtitles.
- **Light Gray** (`#E5E7EB`): Borders, dividends.
- **Off White** (`#F8FAFC`): Alternating section backgrounds.

## 4. Typography
### Web Fonts (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: `Montserrat` (Bold 700 / SemiBold 600).
    - H1: 48–56px (Bold)
    - H2: 32–40px (SemiBold)
- **Body**: `Inter` (Regular 400 / Medium 500).
    - Body: 16–18px.
    - Buttons: 14–16px (SemiBold).

### Print Fallback
- `Montserrat` + `Arial` (if Inter is unavailable).

## 5. Visual Style
- **Photography**: Clean offices, lobbies, bright floors. Uniformed staff with gloves. No "messy" before photos unless necessary for comparison.
- **Iconography**: Linear strokes, simple, consistent. Topics: Hygiene, Insurance, Checklists.
- **Graphics**: Subtle grid lines, rounded corners (12-16px), soft shadows.

## 6. Voice & Tone
- **Tone**: Professional, direct, reliable. Compliance-focused.
- **Key Phrases**:
    - "Insured & SOP-driven crews"
    - "Quality control checklist on every visit"
    - "Scalable teams for multi-location accounts"
